# The Weaving Tides | 航迹：潮汐绘卷

A Minecraft Modpack based on 1.20.1 Forge

*The Weaving Tides* is a RPG & Adventure Modpack with Magic and Islands. You will explore a uncharted archipelago world, conquer the vast oceans, collect treasures and relics, master diverse and powerful spells, forge your own skills to become a powerful mage.  
Immerse yourself in an alternate magical world during the Age of Exploration. You will play as a budding explorer embarking on a journey to seek treasure and uncover ancient secrets. Explore dungeons, challenge foes, and manage your fleet. Pursue lost wisdom and carve out your own epic legend. The tides are shifting — will you be the one to chart their course?  

「航迹：潮汐绘卷 The Weaving Tides」是一款大型剧情向海岛魔法冒险整合包。你将领略一个崭新的群岛世界，征服广袤的海洋，收集宝藏与遗珍，掌握多样而强大的法术，构筑感悟并成为独当一面的强大魔法使。   

体验大航海时代的架空魔法世界。你将扮演一位新晋探险家，踏上寻找宝藏与探索古老秘密的旅程。探索地牢，挑战强敌，经营船队。寻找失落的智慧，书写你的传奇。
